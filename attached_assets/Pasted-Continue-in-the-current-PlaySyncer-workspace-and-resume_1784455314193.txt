### 1. Update Account

Operation:

PATCH /api/accounts/{accountId}

operationId:

updateAccount

Allowed request fields only:

- psnEmail
- psnPassword
- emailPassword
- onlineId
- birthDate
- familyManagementEmail
- confirmed

Rules:

- at least one editable field is required
- confirmed alone is invalid
- omitted fields remain unchanged
- unknown and immutable fields are rejected
- birthDate must be a real YYYY-MM-DD date
- updated sensitive fields are encrypted again
- updated lookup fields regenerate lookup hashes
- omitted sensitive fields must not be decrypted or encrypted again
- PSN Email and Family Management Email comparisons are case-insensitive
- Online ID is trimmed for storage and compared case-insensitively
- immutable identifiers, gameId, Backup Codes, and Capacities remain unchanged
- the operation is atomic
- the response uses only the safe Account DTO and contains no secrets

Reject at minimum:

- id
- gameId
- accountCode
- displayNumber
- accountNumberPrefix
- accountNumberSeq
- status
- statusOverride
- backupCodes
- createdAt
- updatedAt
- unknown fields

### 2. Status Override

Operation:

PATCH /api/accounts/{accountId}/status-override

operationId:

setAccountStatusOverride

Accept exactly:

- SOLD
- INACTIVE
- null

Rules:

- null clears the override
- AVAILABLE and PARTIALLY_SOLD are rejected
- Capacities and Customer Assignments are not changed
- return the safe Account DTO with recalculated canonical status
- preserve status precedence:

INACTIVE override
> SOLD override
> all capacities finished means SOLD
> all capacities available means AVAILABLE
> otherwise PARTIALLY_SOLD
